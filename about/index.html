Kai Hu

UX Architect

My Story

I am an architect turned UX/UI designer, working across multiple disciplines in user experience and user interface design, product design, architecture and interior design. I am keenly interested in crafting useful and delightful user experiences for the products and services that empower people’s productivity and creativity.

With six years’ experience in architecture design industry at award winning international companies and small innovative design firms, I worked in projects across the United States, China, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, and India, spanning various project typologies and work stages.

From 2014 to 2017, seeking to expand my design field and horizon, I pursued two master’s programs at Purdue University, bridging my interests in Scientific Data Visualization, Human-Centered Design, and Interaction Design. Currently, I am working as a UX Architect at Environmental Systems Research Institute (Esri) for the flagship product ArcGIS Pro.

Being fascinated by the internet of things (IoT), I enjoy experimenting with interactive stuff using Arduino and Raspberry Pi in my spare time.
